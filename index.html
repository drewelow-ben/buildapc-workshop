I. Introduction and Purpose


Welcome! This one-day workshop empowers community members to become refurbished PC producers - by learning how computers are built and maintained while prioritizing renewed equipment. In this hands-on experience, you'll learn to identify essential hardware components, assemble a fully functional PC to take with you, and install an operating system. The course will also equip you with critical technical life skills, including electrical safety, navigating component compatibility, and ensuring proper data destruction during electronics recycling.


  • Environmental Stewardship

    • We prioritize used, renewed and refurbished hardware over new equipment.

  • Building and Maintaining a PC

    • We'll learn all that goes into assembling and configuring a PC from its core hardware components.
